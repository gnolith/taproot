# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in Taproot welcoming and free from
harassment for everyone, regardless of identity, experience, or background.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, assume good faith, give actionable technical feedback, and
accept responsibility for mistakes. Do not harass, threaten, demean, publish
another person's private information, or disrupt collaboration.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project.
Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through GitHub's private
vulnerability-reporting channel. Maintainers may remove content, restrict
participation, or ban contributors when necessary, while protecting reporter
privacy as far as practical.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
